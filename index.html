About the Artist

My Name is Chiee

and welcome to my world where every pixel becomes a reality. I'm a passionate digital artist from the Philippines, 20 years of age. Just brushes, crayons, pencils, and paints have allowed me to discover that creating art is what I love to do and what brings me joy since I was young. An artist is someone who not only paints and draw but also has an enviable mind and a desire to bring the impossible to reality. I began sketching everything from random objects to realistic portraits, but after the pandemic hit, I discovered an interest for anime and PC games. And it became the starting point of me for having an interest in being a Digital artist. I’m a self-taught artist, only my curiosity and motivation help me from where I am right now. I am dedicated to pushing the limits of creativity and capturing the possibilities of the future in an ever-developing digital world.
